I combine data engineering, BI, practical AI and clear documentation. That matters because dashboards fail when the model is unclear, pipelines fail when nobody can maintain them, and AI workflows fail when no one owns the review loop.

My work is strongest where messy data, unclear processes and non-technical stakeholders meet: pipelines with documentation, dashboards with a business narrative, and automation that removes manual work without hiding the logic. I also work AI-first — orchestrating coding agents (Claude Code, Codex, OpenCode) with human review to ship faster without losing rigour. Currently finishing my Data & AI Engineering degree at UAO.
